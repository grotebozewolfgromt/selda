Selda (the game)

Selda

Map of the dungeon

Goal

Find princess Selda in the least amount of steps.
It's dark so you dont see where you walk, so look at the map to find out where you are.
Selda by the way, is the sister of Zelda from Amsterdam, who has has been kidnapped by miserable warlord Harry [only Dutch pronunciation allowed].
She is somewhere in his dungeon for some reason, but you don't know where.
Selda is indicated with "S".

She herself doesn't know either where she is, otherwise you could have asked her.
But then you have to find her first, to ask her where she is.
And you can't because you don't know where she is,
Luckily, otherwise you might break the space-time continuum. (and we can't have that: black hole, mess, yada yada)

Mwoahahahaha, ughe, ughe [evil warlord laughter (on a cold day)]

You are Knight Henk [only Dutch pronunciation allowed], represented with "H"
You are a brave hero with a sword, shield and socks etc., but you forgot your helmet.
So make sure that Henk [Dutch pronunciation] doesn't hit his head.
I tell you this, because you wouldn't have known otherwise.

Please play on a desktop computer with a keyboard (color doesn't matter).
This game was made on the 14th of January 1986 13h06m29s PM, we didn't have iPhones and iPads back then, and it was cold (like I said).

The game is controlled with the cursor keys on your keyboard.
Page needs to have focus for the keys to work (open debug console prevents this)
